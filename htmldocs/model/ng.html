Next Generation TablesÂ¶

As part of developing the next generation Questionmark platform older tables are being superseded by new tables most of which do not have any prefix to their name. These next generation tables are documented here.

Although these tables represent the most up-to-date parts of the data model the need to interact with the directly is limited as they are generally available through deliveryodata. You should always access these tables through the API as that imposes additional business rules and constraints that are not coded into the data model itself.
